Dr.Md.Sabur Khan

Founder & Chairman,Daffodil Family

Mission Man of Bangladesh

Md.Sabur Khan is a businessman of Bangladesh.He was the chairman of Bangladesh Computer Samity (BCS) from 2002 to 2003.On 29 December 2012, he became the chairman of Dhaka Chamber of Commerce & Industry (DCCI).He started his business career as an ICT businessman in the decades of 90.He was also the director of Dhaka Chamber of Commerce from 2004 to 2006.Besides,he also performed his duty as a member of ICT task-force from 2002 to 2003.At the same time he continued his duty in Islamic Chamber of Commerce and Industries,Asian University Presidents' Forum and also in other International organizations.He is the founder and chairman of Board of Directors in Daffodil International University.He has been elected as a member of Board of Directors in the World.

Early life and Education

Sabur Khan was born at 23 January 1965 in Chandpur district.His father Md.Yunus Khan.Sabur Khan passed HSC exam from Chandpur Govt. College and complete masters from Jahangirnagar University.
